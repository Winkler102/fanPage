Bio

Leigh Daniel Avidan (born March 14, 1979), also known by his stage name Danny Sexbang, is an American musician, singer-songwriter, and comedian. Dan is part of the comedic band known as Ninja Sex Party and has done multiple cover songs with the guitar Bros and TWRP. Some of his songs include 'Dinosaur Laser fight' and 'Cool patrol' He has also done cover songs of 'Kiss from a rose' and 'Scarborough Fair'. Outside of his music, he also works with Arin Hanson on the Game Grumps youtube channel and plans on visiting every Stegosaurus fossile in the world.
